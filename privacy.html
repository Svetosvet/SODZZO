Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

SODZZO is a video search tool that lets you search YouTube, Vimeo, and Dailymotion at once and watch results without leaving the page. This page explains what happens with your information when you use it.

What SODZZO stores

SODZZO has no user accounts and no server-side database. The only data it keeps is stored locally in your own browser, using localStorage:

  • Favorites — videos you choose to save, so they're there next time you open SODZZO on this device.
  • History — videos you've opened, kept so you can find them again.

This data never leaves your device or browser. It is not sent to any SODZZO server, because SODZZO doesn't have one that stores user data. Clearing your browser's site data for SODZZO deletes it permanently.

What happens when you search

When you type a search, SODZZO sends your search term directly to the video platforms' own search systems so it can show you results:

  • YouTube — search requests are relayed through a small server-side function so SODZZO's API credentials stay off the public internet; Google/YouTube only sees the search term itself, sent from SODZZO's server. SODZZO's use of this API is governed by the YouTube Terms of Service, and Google's handling of that request is described in the Google Privacy Policy.
  • Vimeo — search requests are relayed through a small server-side function so SODZZO's API credentials stay off the public internet; Vimeo only sees the search term itself. See the Vimeo Privacy Policy.
  • Dailymotion — search requests go directly to Dailymotion's public search API. See the Dailymotion Privacy Policy.

Each platform may independently log requests (such as your IP address) as part of operating their own service — that happens under their privacy policies linked above, not SODZZO's, since SODZZO never sees or stores that information itself.

Watching a video

When you open a video, SODZZO embeds that platform's own player (YouTube, Vimeo, or Dailymotion). Once playback starts, you are interacting directly with that platform's player and subject to its own privacy practices, linked above.

No advertising, no tracking, no third-party analytics

SODZZO does not run ads, does not use tracking cookies, and does not share any data with advertisers or analytics companies.

Changes to this policy
